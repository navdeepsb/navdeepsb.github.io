About Me

Hey! I am Navdeep, you can also call me Navi. I am interested in user-centered design and front-end development, a passion that brought me to University of Michigan's School of Information where I am currently pursuing Master of Science in Information specializing in Human-Computer Interaction. Always fascinated by User Interface design, I discovered my passion for User Experience while working on my startup's website where I had to make key decisions as a front-end developer (who designed the web pages too) to make the experience of a visitor as seamless as possible.

I have come to know that powerful ideas solve problems and that feedback and iteration are an important part of the design process.

I am looking for an internship opportunity in UX design for Summer 2017.
